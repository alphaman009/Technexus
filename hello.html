Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard (5-7 business days), expedited (2-3 business days), and next-day shipping. All orders over ₹5,000 qualify for free standard shipping.

What is your return policy?

We have a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days for a full refund, provided it's in its original condition and packaging.

Do you offer a warranty on your gadgets?

Yes, all our new products come with a one-year manufacturer warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. Refurbished items come with a 90-day warranty.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also track your order status from the "My Orders" section of your account (once logged in).
